Hyun Mog Kim

From athletics to pure mathematics, and from pure mathematics to machine learning, my path has been driven by a desire to understand structure, rigor, and performance.

I am a machine learning researcher and engineer working at the intersection of geometry and generative modeling. During my three years of military service, I worked as a research engineer and later as a research scientist, building deep learning systems from production deployment to peer-reviewed publication.

My current interests sit at the intersection of geometric deep learning, generative models, and equivariant neural architectures. I am drawn to problems where symmetry, structure, and learning meet.

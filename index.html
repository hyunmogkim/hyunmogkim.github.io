Machine Learning Research Engineer

Hyun Mog Kim

I am a machine learning research engineer with a foundation in pure mathematics. I care about mathematical rigour — building models from first principles, grounding design choices in analysis, and understanding why something works before scaling it.

Over the past three years I have designed and shipped end-to-end deep learning systems across very different domains: real-time inference on edge hardware under tight latency budgets, and large-scale generative pipelines for drug discovery. Whether the problem calls for a new architecture or a production deployment, I approach it the same way — from the math up.

Currently I work on geometric deep learning and equivariant architectures. I am always looking for harder problems and sharper tools to solve them.
